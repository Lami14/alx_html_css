F.A.Q.

How does this work?

Our smile courses are structured with video tutorials, practical exercises, and personalized feedback. You'll progress through different levels of smiling techniques.

How long does it take to master smiling?

Most students see significant improvement within 2-4 weeks of consistent practice. Full mastery typically takes 2-3 months depending on your dedication.

Do I need any special equipment?

All you need is a mirror for practice! Our advanced courses may recommend a smartphone for recording your smile progress.

Can I really improve my smile?

Absolutely! Smiling is a skill like any other. With proper guidance and practice, anyone can develop a more confident, authentic smile.
